## Introduction

Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) are transforming industries.

### What is AI?

AI is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines.

- **Natural Language Processing**: Enables machines to understand human language.
- **Computer Vision**: Allows computers to interpret and process visual information.

### Conclusion

AI and ML continue to shape the future, making significant impacts across various sectors.
